# Sales Performance Report — Excel

## Objective
Build a complete sales analysis report using Excel on the 
Superstore dataset covering revenue, profit and regional performance.

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel 365
- Pivot Tables
- SUMIF, COUNTIF, VLOOKUP, INDEX MATCH
- Conditional Formatting
- Charts and Dashboard

## Dataset
Superstore Sales Dataset from Kaggle — 9,994 rows

## What's Inside
- Raw_Data sheet — cleaned data with calculated columns
- Analysis sheet — SUMIF/COUNTIF calculations by category and region
- Dashboard sheet — 4 visual charts for management reporting

## Business Key Insights:
1. The Business generated $2.30 million in total sales with an overall profit margin of 12.47%, indicating that the company is profitable but still has opportunities to improve margin.
2. Technology is the best performing category, contributing the highest sales ($836,154) and profit($145,454), making it the primary revenue and profit driver for the business.
3. Furniture generated strong sales ($741,999) but only 2.49% of profit margin, suggesting pricing, discount or cost related issues that require further investigation.
4. The west region recorded the highest sale ($725,457) and profit($108,418), it also has the highest order count with 3203 indicating strong market performance and making it a key region for future growth.
5. California is the highest revenue generating state, contributing $457,687 in sales, highlighting the importance of company’s strongest individual market.
6. Some transactions show negative profit margins, indicating that excessive discounts or high operating costs may be reducing profitability. Identifying and optimizing these loss-making products can improve overall business performance.
